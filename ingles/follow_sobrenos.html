ABOUT US

The company was created on October 3, 2020 through an injured project by ETEC Professor Basilides de Godoy and began to develop due to the great demand for system constructions throughout the market. The historical context occurred through a pandemic, in which its developers find themselves at a distance, communicating their ideas via the Internet. Innovation Pro Systems aims to maximize its profitability, transparency with the customer, fornece provides total secrecy, fast production, commitment etc. One of the main factors is personalized service for companies one of the best projects for the customer to stand out in the market.

Innovation Pro Systems makes up a team of game enthusiasts, being one of the largest stores specialized in games nationwide, always bringing to the public a wide variety of exclusive products with the best price in the market, valuing the quality, variety and fidelity in the selection of the products we offer, so that our audience finds the excellence that seeks quickly and safely. The main objective is to offer customers a unique shopping experience, with agility, good service, technical assistance where the customer accompanies the repair of their video game on the technical side, always acting with respect, providing the best products in the market, in the best terms and payment conditions, providing satisfaction and pleasure in their purchases.

In addition to our website, you can also buy in our physical store, in the city of São Paulo - SP. No matter where you are, you can always count on our passion for video games and dedication to offer the best to our customers, partners and friends. Welcome to Innovation Pro Systems!
